To Love Inevitably

Francis Adrian Adarna

Jade of any kind can never be a beautiful as you
A modest, and vaguely transparent heart that you've shaped
I will wait forever, no matter how long the queue
Lenient personification of an angel that you've made to gape
Appease in us comes and goes like a ripple
Love lacks as though peace lasts eternity
I, mediocre at best, is destined to make everything cripple
Naived demon like me ever since, has turned a new leaf internally
Naked thought of infatuation we thought was nothing
Elixir-like words was the only thing that kept us together
At a point where to commit is the best thing to cling
I pray that you are and will always be mine, oh, Aether
To neglect, or to commit, you cherish the beauty of light
Is to move like a wave that always comes back to an eternity of bright
