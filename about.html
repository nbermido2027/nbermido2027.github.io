HISTORY

The roots of the amazing evolution of InnoVista Academy can be found in the history of a noble and powerful bloodline of the Cristers family. Crisostomo and Ibarra had two children, Yno and Vhisha. Yno was always silent but very intelligent, Vhisha, on the other hand, was a joyful and happy going person. When they got older, they entered the army and they fought against NutSi Gerwasy. The two always loved arts, so when they died in a war, their parents built an art school in tribute for their children.
