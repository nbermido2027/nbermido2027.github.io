ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to SPACE SHOOTERS: BANG BANG! Our game is slightly inspired by Atari's arcade game ASTEROIDS. The mechanics are simple:

  1. The goal of the game is to shoot the meteors and protect the spaceship to get more points.
  2. Use a mouse or trackpad to move and left-click to shoot missiles.
  3. Have fun!
